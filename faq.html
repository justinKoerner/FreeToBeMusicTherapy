Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a musician to benefit from music therapy?

Absolutely not! Music therapy is not about having a certain level of musical skill. Instead, it is about the experience of being human and exploring music in any way that feels comfortable for you.

How long does each therapy session take?

Sessions may be 30 minutes, 45 minutes, or 1 hour depending on your needs. Please contact us for more information.

What is your approach?

We practice a humanistic, strengths-based and affirming approach. Please visit the "About" page to learn more about our values.

Where do your music therapy sessions take place?

Sessions can take place in your home, your backyard (weather permitting), in a studio space that we rent, or online.
